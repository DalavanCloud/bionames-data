Journal Description

The Journal of the Torrey Botanical Society (until 1997 TheBulletin of the Torrey Botanical Club), the oldest botanical journal inthe Americas, has as its primary goal the dissemination of scientific knowledgeabout plants (in the broad sense of both plants and fungi). It publishes basicresearch in all areas of plant biology except horticulture, with emphasis onresearch done in, and about plants of, the Western Hemisphere.
